Craftsmanship

Made to Last
Centuries

Each FORMA bust begins with fine dental plaster, poured into precision moulds taken from museum-quality originals. After curing for 72 hours, each piece is hand-sanded through five grades to achieve a surface that holds paint with the fidelity of stone.

Our signature lacquer is applied in three coats by hand, each layer dried and lightly abraded before the next. The result is a depth of colour that photographs cannot fully capture — and a surface that only improves with the touch of time.

Every bust leaves our studio individually inspected and signed on the base. These are objects made to be passed down.
